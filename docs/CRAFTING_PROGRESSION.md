# Crafting Progression Guide

## Tier 0: Survival & Scavenging
**Key Resources:** `Wood Stick`, `Sharp Stone`, `Plant Fibers`, `Scrap Metal`.
**Activities:**
- Foraging forests for wood and fibers.
- Scavenging junk piles for scrap metal.
- Crafting primitive tools (`Knife`, `Club`, `Cordage`).

## Tier 1: Basic Processing
**Key Resources:** `Charcoal`, `Cooked Meat`, `Leather`, `Wood Planks`.
**Facilities:** `Campfire`, `Workbench`.
**Activities:**
- **Cooking:** Safely prepare raw meat and water.
- **Leatherworking:** Turn `Raw Hide` -> `Rawhide` -> `Cured Leather` -> `Leather Strips`.
- **Woodworking:** Saw logs into planks for construction.
- **Chemistry:** Burn wood for `Charcoal` (fuel/filtering).

## Tier 2: Metalworking & Chemistry
**Key Resources:** `Iron Ingot`, `Copper Ingot`, `Gunpowder`, `Lye`.
**Facilities:** `Forge`, `Anvil`, `Chemistry Set`.
**Activities:**
- **Smelting:** Process `Iron Ore` or `Scrap Metal` into Ingots.
- **Forging:** Create durable metal tools (`Hammer`, `Tongs`) and weapon parts.
- **Chemistry:** Mix `Sulfur`, `Charcoal`, and `Saltpeter` for `Gunpowder`. Leech `Wood Ash` for `Lye`.

## Tier 3: Advanced Manufacturing
**Key Resources:** `Steel Ingot`, `Plastic Scrap (Processed)`, `Electronic Components`.
**Facilities:** `Machine Shop`, `Electronics Bench`.
**Activities:**
- **Machining:** Create precision parts (`Gears`, `Bolts`, `Engine Blocks`).
- **Electronics:** Salvage `Electronic Scrap` into circuits and wires. Craft sensors and motors.
- **Vehicles:** Assemble `Chassis`, `Wheels`, and `Engines` into functional transport.
- **Ammunition:** Reload spent casings with primers and powder.
